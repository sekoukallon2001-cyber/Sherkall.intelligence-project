Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

⚡ How long does installation take? ▾

About 45 to 60 minutes. Our team can come to your home or office in Conakry. No mechanical changes to your vehicle.

📶 Does it work outside Conakry? ▾

Yes. Coverage spans all major regions in Guinea and Sierra Leone, with no extra roaming fees.

📱 Do I need a smartphone? ▾

Yes. The platform works on Android, iPhone, or any web browser. As long as you have internet, you can see your vehicle.

🔒 Is my data private? ▾

Absolutely. Your vehicle data is encrypted and only you can access it. We never share your information with anyone.

💳 How do I pay? ▾

You can pay via Orange Money, MTN Mobile Money, bank transfer, or cash. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange payment.

🚗 What vehicles can be tracked? ▾

Any vehicle with a 12V battery: cars, SUVs, trucks, minibuses, taxis, motorcycles. We have solutions for every type.
